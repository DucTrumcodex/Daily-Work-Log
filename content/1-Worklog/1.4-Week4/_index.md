### Week 4 Achievements:

* IAM fundamentals:
  * Clearly understood the roles of User, Group, Role, and Policy in AWS access management.
  * Can distinguish when to use User/Group versus Role for assuming permissions.

* Secure permission design:
  * Set up permissions using least privilege, granting only what each task needs.
  * Practiced writing/attaching policies and verifying access via Console/CLI.

  * Enabled MFA for important users to reduce account takeover risk.
  * Reviewed and removed excess permissions after labs to avoid leaving unsafe configs.
